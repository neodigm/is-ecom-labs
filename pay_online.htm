    Privacy

    LTD Commodities LLC holds the privacy and security of our customers in the highest regard. The following data explains what kind of information is gathered on the LTD Commodities website and how the information is used.

    LTD Commodities LLC will treat your email address as confidential information; we will not rent, sell or trade the contents of our email list to any outside parties.

     

    Does LTD Commodities offer secure online ordering?

    Yes, we do. All customer and order information is securely stored behind a firewall and protected from unauthorized access. Your online session is encrypted using SSL with a certificate provided by Verisign. Therefore, none of your personal information, including credit card number, name and address, can be read as the information travels over the internet. Your credit card number is protected by our encryption methods as soon as we capture it and before it is stored in our database to protect it from potential theft. LTD Commodities does not store your CVV2 credit card security code anywhere on its system.

     

    What do you do with my email address?

    LTD Commodities LLC collects your email address as part of your registration with us. We use this address to communicate with you about your account. We do not sell or otherwise give out your email address to others.

    What kind of information do you collect from me when I visit your web site?

    We do not collect any information from those merely visiting our site. We do ask for additional information if you would like to place an order, request an order status or a free catalog, or if you need to communicate with us regarding an account issue.

    Does LTD share information it collects from me with outside parties?

    The information you provided on the web site is used to fulfill a product order, a catalog request or research a customer inquiry. The information that you provide is stored in house separate from the Internet.

    We do occasionally share name and address information with other catalog companies that offer similar products. We will not share any personal financial information. If you are on our catalog mailing list, or order from one of our catalogs, and would prefer not to have your name shared with other catalog companies, please email at MarketingDepartment@ltdcommodities.com and ask that your name not be shared.

    From time to time, we may modify or amend this privacy and security policy in order to comply with new laws or regulations or to reflect future changes in our business practices. All such modifications and amendments to the privacy and security policy shall be posted here in the Customer Service section of www.ltdcommodities.com. The current policy has been in effect since May 2009.

    How else do you use information about my company?

    Once you make a purchase from us, your company becomes a part of our customer file. As a valued LTD Commodities customer, we will add you to our mailing list to receive future catalogs and offers.

    How do I remove my company name from LTD's mailing lists?

     Contact us by email, mail or fax and we will remove you from our mailing list. Please include the company account number, company name and address, and your name and title.
