Do I owe sales tax on my purchase?

LakesideLTD is not required to and does not collect sales or use taxes in all states. We currently collect sales tax only to orders shipped within Illinois. Sales tax for Illinois orders should be calculated at 7.5% of total, including merchandise subtotal and shipping charges.


For all other states imposing sales or use taxes, your purchase is subject to sales/use tax unless it is specifically exempt from taxation. Your purchase is not exempt merely because it is made over the Internet, by catalog or other remote means. You are still responsible for tax on your purchase. Most states require purchasers to file a use tax return to report any purchase that was not taxed and pay tax on the purchase. Details of how to report and pay tax to your state may be found at your state taxing authority's website. In addition, the states of Colorado, Kentucky, Oklahoma, South Dakota and Vermont require us to provide the following additional information:



Kentucky
For Kentucky purchasers: LakesideLTD is not required to and does not collect Kentucky sales or use tax. Your purchase may be subject to Kentucky use tax unless the purchase is exempt from tax in Kentucky. The purchase is not exempt merely because it is made over the Internet, by catalog or by other remote means. The Commonwealth of Kentucky requires Kentucky purchasers to report all purchases of tangible personal property or digital property that are not taxed by the retailer and pay use tax on those purchases unless exempt under Kentucky law. The tax may be reported and paid on the Kentucky individual income tax return or by filing a consumer use tax return with the Kentucky Department of Revenue. These forms and corresponding instructions may be found on the Kentucky Department of Revenue's Internet Web site at www.revenue.ky.gov/individual/usetax.

Oklahoma
For Oklahoma purchasers, the tax may be reported and paid on the Oklahoma individual income tax return or by filing a consumer use tax return. The referenced forms and corresponding instructions are available on the Oklahoma Tax Commissioner website at www.tax.ok.gov.

South Dakota
For South Dakota purchasers, the tax may be reported and paid on the South Dakota use tax form. The use tax form and corresponding instructions are available from the South Dakota Department of Revenue website at www.state.sd.us/drr/.

Vermont
For Vermont purchasers, the tax may be reported and paid on the Vermont use tax form. The use tax form and corresponding instructions are available from the Vermont Department of Revenue website at www.state.vt.us/tax/usetax.

Colorado
For Colorado purchasers, the tax may be reported and paid on the Colorado use tax form. Details of how to file this return may be found at the Colorado Department of Revenue's website at www.taxcolorado.com.

Pay Your Way is an alternative payment option offered to select Lakeside Customers. This option offers this pre-selected group of customers the option of breaking up their credit card payment into 4 equal installments on eligible orders. This promotion is subject to change at any time.

How do I become eligible for the installment plan payment options on Lakeside?

Presently, Lakeside is offering the installment payment option to a limited number of existing pre-qualified Lakeside Customers by invitation only. We hope to be able to offer this to a broader number of customers very soon.

I placed an order using the installment plan payment option but I don't understand how it works?

The installment plan payment option allows eligible customers to pay for their purchase using 4 monthly installment payments. It works like this:

  • You place an order for merchandise over $100.

  • When your in-stock merchandise ships and you are billed for 25% of the order, as well as 25% of any applicable shipping and taxes.

  • 30 days later the credit card you used at the time of the order will automatically bill for the next balance due of 25% of your shipment. This will happen again once a month until your bills is paid in full?usually a total of 90 days from the time the order shipped.

  • If any items were on back order or shipped direct from one of our manufacturers, your order may split into additional installment payments. Each of these separate shipments will bill in its own 4 installment payment plan.

  • You will never be billed more than your original order total.

I used my installment plan once, and I'd like to place another order using it. How come I don't see this option at check out?

The Lakeside Collection reserves the right to offer different promotions and payment options at any time, and may remove this option on new or subsequent orders. Should you have further questions, please refer to our Terms and Conditions or contact a credit representative at 847-444-3188.

I just received a charge to my card but it is for a lot less than the 25% I was expecting?

Please check your order status to determine if only a portion of your order shipped. If so, you will be billed in a separate installment payment plan for the remaining items, as they ship. This will mean you will have several smaller payments due to us all 30 days from each shipment date. However, the total amount paid will never exceed your original order value.

What are the guidelines for writing a Product Review?

When writing a review, other customers will find it most helpful if you stay focused on the specific reasons you like or dislike the product you've purchased. Make sure you keep it easy-to-read and concise, preferably 75-300 words (there is a 50 character minimum).

In addition, your review will not be published if it contains profanity or other offensive language, mentions competitors or the specific price you paid for the product, or personally identifiable information (such as your full name, phone number, etc.)

Your feedback is important to us, and writing a product review is a great way to let us know what products you like or dislike, and why. However, to provide feedback regarding service, shipping, or other non-product information, please Contact Us rather than submitting them through product ratings and reviews.

Can my review be blocked or removed?

Yes, while LakesideLTD Commodities does value both positive and negative customer feedback regarding our products and services, and is committed to posting product reviews in order to help our customers make educated buying decisions, we do reserve the right block or remove a product review for any reason. To help ensure we are able to post your review, please make your review does not include any of the following:

  • Profanity, obscenities, inappropriate comments or other language that might be deemed offensive.
  • Comments regarding our competitors or competitive products, offers, or websites.
  • Any form of "spam", including political campaigning, commercial solicitation, chain letters, mass mailings, etc.
