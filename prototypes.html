Authenticated user

This "above the fold" design utilizes the Collapsable Content Card pattern. The cards are mutually exclusive, meaning that each card can be open or closed individually, but opening one card will close any other open card. The first card will initially be open. The cards are asynchronous. When it is submitted it will temporarily display a spinner and a message (toast) but the page will not refresh. The card will disappear after it has been submitted then the next card in the sequence will be opened. The current thinking is that the user’s participation in Installment Payments will have no effect on the post checkout experience.
